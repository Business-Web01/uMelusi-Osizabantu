Our Programs & Initiatives

Workshops & Mentorship

Our exciting Anti-Bullying 2 days Workshops, will help RCL, SGB, Educators and Parents, to understand bullying, how to respond and how to support others.

Our exciting Leadership 2 days Workshops, will help participants (RCL, SGB and Educators) develop critical thinking, confidence, and essential life skills to become leaders in their communities.

After these workshops we will appoint some learners to be ambassadors called ‘ABASIZI’, who will promote anti-bullying in their schools and be our direct contact.

We will also issue Attendance Certificates.

Counseling Support

Provide conflict resolution support, particularly in cases of school bullying, to restore harmony and promote understanding.

School Visits

Educate learners about the consequences of crime, human rights, and personal accountability while fostering essential life skills.

Support learners in managing emotions, overcoming insecurities, and coping with stress and anxiety.

Inspire individuals to overcome challenges, build resilience, and embrace a positive outlook on life.
