Anti Bullying Campaign

Date: June 04, 2024

Time: 9AM - 11AM

Location: House of Paradise Woman & Children Multi-Purpose Center Sunrise Katlehong

Child Protection Week at House of Paradise was a great success, bringing together key stakeholders and community members in a united effort to advocate for the safety and well-being of children. In collaboration with our government partners—SAPS, GDE, Correctional Services, and the City of Ekurhuleni—we worked to raise awareness about children's rights and the importance of creating a protective environment for them. The event was not only about education but also about taking action to support vulnerable children and their families.

As part of our commitment to making a tangible difference, we donated clothes and blankets to ensure that children in need have access to warmth and comfort. Beyond material support, we provided emotional and educational guidance, with forum members offering words of encouragement to parents. They were reminded of the critical laws in place to safeguard children from neglect, abuse, and exploitation. Through these discussions, we emphasized the role of both caregivers and the broader community in upholding these protections.

Moving forward, we aim to strengthen our partnerships and continue advocating for child protection. By hosting more outreach programs, workshops, and awareness campaigns, we hope to empower families with the knowledge and resources needed to create a safer and more nurturing environment for children. Our work does not end with this event—it is a stepping stone toward long-term positive change in our communities.
