Anti Bullying Campaign

Date: January 30, 2025

Location: Izibuko Primary School

In addition to the theater play, we facilitated open discussions where students could share their thoughts, experiences, and concerns regarding bullying and teenage pregnancy. Educators and community leaders also participated, providing valuable insights and guidance on how young learners can protect themselves and support one another. By fostering a safe and interactive environment, we encouraged students to ask questions and engage in meaningful conversations, ensuring they felt heard and empowered.

This initiative is just the beginning of our commitment to creating a positive impact in schools across Katlehong. We plan to expand our outreach to more schools, incorporating workshops, mentorship programs, and continued theatrical performances to reinforce these important messages.
