Anti Bullying Campaign

Date: June 30, 2024

Time: 10AM - AM

Location: The Zion Apostolic Church In Jerusalem (Green Grass) 489 Moagi Street Vosloorus

On the 30th of June, we will be celebrating Youth Month at Green Grass Church in Vosloorus, bringing together young people, community leaders, and key stakeholders for a day of empowerment and inspiration. In collaboration with Correctional Services, SAPS, EMPD, the City of Ekurhuleni, Green Grass Church, and the Gauteng Department of Education (GDE), this event will serve as a platform to address critical issues affecting today’s youth. Our goal is to equip young people with knowledge, guidance, and opportunities that will help them make positive life choices and contribute meaningfully to their communities.

The event will focus on key topics such as the reintegration of ex-convicts, emphasizing the importance of second chances and support systems in helping former offenders rebuild their lives. Discussions on the value of education, crime prevention, and the dangers of substance abuse will highlight the risks young people face and how they can safeguard their futures. Additionally, we will explore the power of entrepreneurship, encouraging youth to pursue self-sufficiency and economic independence. Through motivational talks and interactive sessions, we will also stress the importance of using one’s gifts and talents to create opportunities and inspire change.

We believe that by fostering these conversations and providing valuable resources, we can empower young people to take charge of their futures with confidence. This Youth Month celebration is more than just an event—it is a call to action for the youth of Vosloorus to rise above challenges and embrace their potential. We look forward to seeing the impact of this initiative as we continue to support and uplift our young leaders of tomorrow.
